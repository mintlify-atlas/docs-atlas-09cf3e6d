BestClient is a customized DDNet client built on top of TClient, engineered around native BestClient systems rather than a thin reskin. It ships with its own rendering stack, competitive input tools, social systems, live HUD tooling, fullscreen editors, an in-client asset shop, and a curated Fun tab — all without leaving the DDNet ecosystem.

## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download BestClient">
    Grab the latest release from [bestclient.fun](https://bestclient.fun) for your platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch and connect">
    Open BestClient, browse servers in the Server Browser, and connect to any DDNet server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the BestClient settings tab">
    Open Settings → BestClient to find all feature toggles organized by category: Visuals, Input, Social, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your HUD">
    Press the HUD Editor bind (or open it from settings) to drag music player, voice, and chat modules to your preferred positions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
